基础面板使用

一个完整的面板包括头、内容和底。

    <div class="panel-heading">标题</div>
    <div class="panel-body">内容</div>
    <div class="panel-footer">底部</div>
</div>

可收缩面板

利用 collapse ，实现可收缩。 


<div class="panel panel-default">
    <div class="panel-heading" data-toggle="collapse" data-target="#collapseExample1">默认折叠</div>
    <div class="panel-body collapse" id="collapseExample1">内容</div>
</div>
<div class="panel panel-default">
    <div class="panel-heading" data-toggle="collapse" data-target="#collapseExample2">默认展开</div>
    <div class="panel-body in" id="collapseExample2">内容</div>
</div>

可收缩面板

利用 collapse ，实现可收缩。 


<div class="panel-group" id="accordion">
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapseOne">
            Panel #1
        </div>
        <div id="collapseOne" class="panel-collapse collapse in">
            <div class="panel-body">
                内容一
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading" id="headingTwo" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapseTwo">
            Panel #2
        </div>
        <div id="collapseTwo" class="panel-collapse collapse">
            <div class="panel-body">
                内容二
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="panel panel-default">
        <div class="panel-heading" id="headingThree" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapseThree">
            Panel #3
        </div>
        <div id="collapseThree" class="panel-collapse collapse">
            <div class="panel-body">
                内容三
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
